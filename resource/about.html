What is Kivodle?

A game where you guess a playable student (character) from the mobile game "Blue Archive". Use clues based on the student's traits to deduce the correct answer.

How to Play

  1. Select a student from the dropdown list and click "Guess".
  2. The game compares your chosen student with the correct answer across five categories: "Weapon", "Class", "School", "Attack Type", and "Release Date".
    A green background indicates a match, while a red background indicates a mismatch. For "Release Date", you'll also see whether the correct student's release date is before or after your chosen student's.
  3. Deduce the correct student within five guesses based on the clues from step 2 to win the game. Failing to do so results in a game over.

Game Modes

Daily Mode

The correct answer changes daily and is the same for all players.
The answer resets at 4:00 AM Japan Standard Time (19:00 UTC).
Share your results just like the original Wordle!
Your game progress is automatically saved in your browser even if you close the tab or browser mid-game.

Endless Mode

The correct answer changes after each game, and your win streak is recorded.
Share your win streak when you get a wrong answer and the game ends.
Your game progress is automatically saved in your browser even if you close the tab or browser mid-game.

Speedrun Mode

The correct answer changes after each game, and the time taken to solve 10 questions is recorded.
The game doesn't end even if you get a wrong answer.
The time between the answer reveal and clicking "Next Question" is not recorded.
Please note that closing the browser or tab during this mode will discard your current progress.
